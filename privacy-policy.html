Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect the information you share with us.

Last updated: May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EA Analytics ("EA Analytics", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you interact with us, including when you respond to our advertisements on Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram), submit a lead form, contact us, or book a call. By sharing your information with us, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Who We Are

EA Analytics is an analytics consultancy operated by Elisha Amir, based in Tel Aviv and working with clients globally. For any questions about this policy or your personal data, contact us at info@ea-analytics.com.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect the information needed to respond to you and tell you about our services.

Information you provide

  • Phone number — the primary detail we collect through our lead forms and advertising.
  • Email address — optional, collected so we have an additional way to reach you.
  • Name — where provided.
  • Company and message details — if you fill out our contact form or write to us directly.

Information collected automatically

  • Advertising and analytics data — when you interact with our Meta ads or website, we and our advertising partners may collect technical data such as your device type, browser, IP address, and the actions you take, using cookies and similar technologies (see Section 7).
  • Log data — standard information your browser sends when you visit our site, such as pages viewed and timestamps.

3. How We Collect It

  • Meta lead forms — when you submit your details through an ad on Facebook or Instagram, Meta shares that information with us.
  • Our website — through the contact form on ea-analytics.com (processed by our form provider, Web3Forms) and our online booking link.
  • Direct contact — when you email us or message us.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you give us to:

  • Contact you by phone call or text message (SMS) in response to your inquiry;
  • Reach out by email where you have provided an email address;
  • Communicate with you via WhatsApp where appropriate;
  • Send you information, updates, and marketing communications about our services;
  • Respond to your questions and schedule calls or meetings;
  • Improve our services and the performance of our advertising.

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by replying STOP to a text, replying "unsubscribe" to an email, or emailing info@ea-analytics.com. We will still be able to contact you about a request you have specifically made.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Where data protection laws such as the GDPR apply, we rely on the following legal bases: your consent (which you give when you submit your details through our ads or forms and which you may withdraw at any time); our legitimate interests in responding to inquiries and promoting our services; and, where relevant, steps taken at your request before entering into an agreement.

6. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Service providers that help us operate, such as Meta Platforms (advertising and lead forms), Web3Forms (contact form processing), Google (calendar booking and fonts), and the messaging and email tools we use to contact you;
  • Legal and regulatory authorities, where required by law or to protect our rights.

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We and our advertising partners use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies — including the Meta Pixel — to measure ad performance, understand how people use our site, and show relevant advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings and manage ad preferences through the settings on Facebook and Instagram. Disabling cookies may affect how parts of our site work.

8. Data Retention

We keep your personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy — typically while we are in contact with you and for a reasonable period afterward — unless a longer retention period is required by law. When it is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information, to object to certain processing, to withdraw consent, and to request a copy of your data. To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@ea-analytics.com and we will respond in line with applicable law.

10. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International Transfers

We operate internationally, and your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including by the service providers listed above. Where required, we take steps to ensure your data receives an adequate level of protection.

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version. Material changes will be posted on this page.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at info@ea-analytics.com.
