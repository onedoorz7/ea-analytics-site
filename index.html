Questions I hear on every first call

"We don't think we have enough data."
If you have a CRM and 6+ months of marketing data, you have enough. A quick discovery call will tell us where you stand.
"How is this different from a BI tool like Looker?"
Looker and Tableau show you what happened. I tell you what to do about it.
"What tools do you work with?"
Whatever you use. Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Analytics, spreadsheets. I work with your existing stack.
"We can't afford a data analyst right now."
A full-time junior analyst costs $6K-$8K/mo. I cost less and the output is senior level. Start and stop based on need.
"Who would we actually be working with?"
Me, directly. No handoffs, no juniors. The best way to get a feel for how I think is my LinkedIn. I post the way I work.
"We're planning to hire someone eventually."
I build the foundation: clean data, scoring models, dashboards. So your future hire hits the ground running.
