Case Study • Series A B2B SaaS • ~40 Employees

How a Series A startup stopped guessing and started prioritizing with data

Challenge

Marketing team generating leads but no way to tell which ones would convert. SDRs spending equal time on every lead. No clarity on which channels actually drove pipeline.

What We Did

Built a lead scoring model from Salesforce data. Refined their ICP based on actual conversion patterns. Created pipeline attribution showing each marketing channel's true ROI.

Results

Team now prioritizes leads with confidence. Marketing spend allocated to channels that actually convert. ICP definition backed by data, not assumptions. Clearer pipeline visibility for leadership.

"We went from making gut-feeling decisions to having real data behind every GTM move. The lead scoring alone changed how our entire sales team operates."
